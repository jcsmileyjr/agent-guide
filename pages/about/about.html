About

What is this app?

A field guide for gaming enforcement agents. An modern day method of digitally searching the gaming regulations by short practical topics/solutions to re-occurring problems.

Why build it?

MGC needed a tool to list all the potential ways to apply the gaming regulation to violations found in the casino. A cheat-sheet to make writing violations easier. A quick reminder of past strategies and battle tested tactics that have proven useful based on certain situation.
